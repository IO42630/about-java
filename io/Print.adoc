=== PrinStream
* System.out and System.err are PrintStream

=== PrintWriter
* PrintWriter(Outputstream)

* both inherit .write from Writer (but this throws Exception )
* also have
** print()
** println()
** format() methods, which do not throw exceptions
* both have
** .checkError() to detect presence of problem after attempted write

* print()
** overloaded  with all primitives / String / Object
** generally performs String.valueOf()




* println()
** print() + linebreak
** usefull as linebreak may depend on JVM
** System.getProperty("line.separator);


* format() alias for printf()
